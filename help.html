User Manual For Grievance Monitoring System


  1. To Log -on to Grievances Monitoring System of PCMC visit the following website through the url
    2. http://203.129.227.15:8086/Grievance/citizen/
    1. For registering to the site click on the Citizen Registration.
    2. If you are a registered citizen then enter your username (Mobile no) and password
    3. The Citizen Registration Page will appear.
      • For registering to the site, enter your Mobile No or Email and click on the Save button.
      • The fields in red (First name, Last Name) are necessary fields.
      • If you want to clear the form then click on the reset button.
      • If you want to go back to the home page then click on the home link.
      • After saving the information you will receive a text message on your mobile phone from PCMC containing your password.
      • You can use this password to login to the site (remember your username will be your Mobile No).
    4. Whenr click on the Save button “ Register Grievance” Page will appear.
      • You can enter your Grievance through this screen.
      • The subject and description are compulsory.
      • Click on the Save button to save the grievance.
      • If you want to clear the form then click on Reset button.
      • Click on My Grievances link to view the history.
    5. Using this screen user can see their grievance history.
      • If you want to enter a new grievance then click on the New Grievance link.
      • On the Top Panel you see your Grievance along with the Token Number.
      • When you click on the Subject of the Grievance, you can see details on it within the Panels at the Bottom.
      • On the Bottom Left Panel you see a detailed History of how your Grievance has moved within the Department at PCMC.
      • On the Bottom Right Panel you see the last comment on your Grievance.
    6. If You want to update your profile then click on Update Profile link.
    7. The Citizen Registration Screen will appear.
    8. To register your complaint click on the Register Your Complaint Link
    9. The Log On screen will appear. Your username will be your Mobile No .
    10. When click on Log On button You Can see your grievance history record

    11. Click on the Check Complaint Status to see your grievance status.

    12. Enter your Token Number and click on Show button to know your status.

    13. Show Grievance Screen will appear.

    14. Through this screen you can know your status

    15. Click on Change Password link “Change Password Screen” will appear

      • If you want to change the password you have to enter your original password, new password and confirm the new password.
      • If you want to change your password then click on the Change Password link.
      • If you want to change the password you have to enter your original password, new password and confirm the new password.
    16. This screen confirms you “Your Password is changed”

    17. Click on the Forgot Password link to get your password on your Mobile number or Email id. If you forgot your password then click on the Forgot Password link.

    18. Enter your Mobile Number or Email ID to know your new password.
