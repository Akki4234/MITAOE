About eSuvidha

eSuvidha is a Suite of eServices offered by the Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Corporation to its Citizens.
The Mission:
To bring to every citizen of PCMC the benefits of advances in technology by identifying, harnessing and employing state-of-the-art systems in city administration...
The Suite:
eServices under the eSuvidha initiative cover all aspects of the working of the Municipal Corporation
Citizens Services:

The following Citizens Services are part of the eSuvidha Suite.

  • Birth & Death Registration
  • Water & Sewerage
  • Solid Waste Management
  • Town Planning & Development
  • Public Hospitals
  • Health Program Management
  • Sports Facilities
  • Public Schools
  • Basic Services for Urban Poor
  • Citizens Facility Centers
  • Information Portal & Kiosks
Income Department Services:
  • Octroi
  • Property Tax
  • Water Tax
  • Building Plan Approval
  • Rent from Land & Estates
  • Income from PPP & BOT projects
  • Other Incomes
    • Services for Public Works:

    The following Services for the Public Works Departments within PCMC are part of the eSuvidha Suite.

    1. Civil Works
      • Roads, Bridges, Public Buildings
    2. Water & Sewerage Works
      • Pipelines, Sewerage Treatment Plants
    3. Electrical Works
      • Electrification of Highways, Streets & Public Buildings
      • Electrification of Pumping Stations and STPs
    Support Establishment Services:

    The following Services for the Support Establishment within PCMC are part of the eSuvidha Suite.


    • Leadership, Strategy & Planning
    • Personnel & Administration
    • Accounts
    • Audit
    • Stores & Inventory Management
    • Information Technology
    • Document, File & Content Management
